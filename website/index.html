Who We Are

A generation building
a better future.

Better Nature is a youth-led 501(c)(3) nonprofit on a mission to create lasting environmental and social impact through community action.

Founded by students who believe young people can drive real change, we operate through volunteer chapters at high schools and colleges nationwide. Each chapter runs local programs in food rescue, habitat conservation, and clean water access.

Our model is simple: empower volunteers with the tools, training, and community they need to make a measurable difference — then track every meal rescued, every hour volunteered, and every dollar raised with full transparency.

01

Youth-Led

Students lead every chapter, making decisions and driving impact in their own communities.

02

Transparent

Every meal, hour, and dollar is tracked and reported. 100% of donations fund programs.

03

Scalable

Our chapter model lets us grow to any school, city, or state without losing local focus.

Get Involved

How it works

From signing up to making an impact — it's simple.

1

Download & Sign Up

Download the Better Nature app, create your account, and join your school's chapter — or start your own.

2

Join Events & Pick Up

Browse upcoming events, sign up, and claim food pickups from partner restaurants. See who's going and get directions in the app.

3

Check In & Log Impact

Your chapter president checks you in at events. Hours, meals rescued, and contributions are automatically logged to your profile.

4

Track & Grow

Watch your impact grow on the leaderboard. Earn badges, see org-wide stats, and compete with other chapters.
